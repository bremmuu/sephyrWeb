Welcome to Domain Sephyr




A collection of souls—heroes and foes alike—bound by fate and legend. We embark on their journey through light and shadow, set in an alternate history of the British Isles. Amidst the rise of tyranny, outcast Celts, once forgotten and overshadowed by the dominion of British supremacy, rise against oppression. Through valor and sacrifice, they forge their own destiny, fighting to shatter the chains of conquest and reclaim a future worthy of their ancestors.
